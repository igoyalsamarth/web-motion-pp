BROWSER MOTION

Privacy Policy

Vim-style keyboard control for your browser

Browser Motion is a browser extension that lets you create domain-specific hotkeys to control your browser entirely with the keyboard. You can assign shortcuts to DOM clicks or URL navigation—think vim-motion meets browser automation. This policy explains how we handle your data.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What Browser Motion Does

Browser Motion enables you to:

  • Create custom hotkeys that work on specific domains
  • Assign keystrokes to trigger DOM element clicks
  • Map shortcuts to URL routes for quick navigation
  • Control your browser with keyboard-only, vim-motion style workflows

Data We Collect

None. Browser Motion does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or browsing activity. We have no analytics, no tracking, and no servers that receive your information.

Data Stored Locally

The extension uses your browser's local storage to save your configurations—your custom hotkeys, domain mappings, and shortcut assignments. This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never sent to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data

Permissions We Use

Browser Motion requests certain browser permissions to function. Here's why:

  • activeTab — To read and interact with the current page when you press a hotkey (e.g., triggering a click on a specific element)
  • storage — To save your hotkey configurations locally
  • scripting — To execute your assigned actions (clicks, navigation) on web pages when you invoke shortcuts
  • host_permissions — To apply your domain-specific rules only on the sites you've configured

These permissions are used solely to power the extension's features. We do not access pages you haven't explicitly configured, and we do not log or transmit any page content.

Third Parties

Browser Motion does not integrate with any third-party services. No ad networks, no analytics SDKs, no external APIs that receive your data.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll post the revised version here and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Browser Motion, please reach out through the extension's support channel or the contact information provided in the extension listing.
